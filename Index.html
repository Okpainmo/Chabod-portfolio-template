Project Name: Releef
Releef got started at a Google DSC(developer student club) event that I attended at Bida polytechnic, Niger state, Nigeria. The event was a three day event (two workshop days + a one day hackathon). The hackathon was to build a solution that would compete in the Google solution Challenge 2021. The solution Challenge this year (2021), was for teams to provide solutions to some global issues like gender equality, poverty and etcetera.
At the event, Everyone was asked to pitch ideas in any of the listed problem areas that we could build a solution on. That was when I got the idea to build Releef - The Releef spark came up when i was in the bathroom (i think i was bathing then). - funny right?
I then Shared the Releef idea at the Event. and we started out - building it.
Releef was to compete in the "zero poverty category".
Releef is simply a platform where people can share real and verifiable information about regions in the world that are suffering from hardships like famines, wars, and the main enemy - poverty. On Releef, people would post written details about such locations, along with clear Images. Releef was also meant to provide a Google map interface for users to supply map details of such locations. Another major feature of Releef was to be "Donation" - A payment gateway was to be provided for users to make donations to any locations of their choice.
Releef would then partner with trusted bodies like UNICEF, UNHRC and others. For proper distribution of such donations among other benefits.
